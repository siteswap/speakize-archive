Include in archive the history of the project:

30th July 2020:
 - First git commit - a python script that calls Google TTS & Translate to produce Anki audio flashcards
 - Struggling to find relevant content, rely on online lessons & HSK website
July 2021 - passed HSK4 (considered 1 year of full time work):
 - It's still just python scripts, now covering HSK1-4
 - I've read Fluent Forever and started including word frequency metadata, but no images yet
Nov 2021:
 - First Django site commit
 - Try to solve the content / relevance problem with word tracking, word feed, hanzi newsfeed, youtube imports etc. 
Nov 2022:
 - By now the site has developed most of it's major features
 - 22nd Nov to 22nd Dec 2022 - learn first 1000 Spanish words in 1 month (word feed) to test site with a new lang
Jan 2023 - Aug 2023:
 - Professional reviews, Reddit user feedback, searched for PMF, Twinkle (funding/feedback) 
 - A few features based on feedback - new front end, 20+ langs to grab more users, UX/usability, shared content for instant start
 - Found this period frustrating; lots of time passing without clear progress, UX/front-end never quite comes together
 - Language wise, probably around HSK5 by now (considered another 1 year of full time work)
 - Last features/bug fixes added on Sept 6th 2023

In total - 3 years of work in parrallel with chinese study

March 2024 - Cost reduction work (spins up getting hit by bots & fake accounts)
April 2026 - Archived as a static site
