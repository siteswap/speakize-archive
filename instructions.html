How it works

Speakize optimizes language learning. Set your goals in the Settings page. Then use the Smart Feed to study efficiently.


1. Settings

Start at the Settings page to set your target language and the initial number of words to learn.

2. AI smart feed

The Smart Feed provides a personalized media feed, optimized for your learning goals using spaced repetition and word tracking.

3. One-click flashcard creation

Click on any word in your target language to open a popup window. Then click , or to create a flashcard.

4. Add lesson notes

Navigate to the Documents page and click "Add Text". Paste in text from your lesson notes, provide a title, and click Save.

5. Add YouTube content

Navigate to Documents and click "Add YouTube". Paste a YouTube link and click Save. Subtitles will be fetched automatically.

6. Find more word examples

Click on the "more_info" link in the word popup. The phrases you see are personalized for your specific learning goals.

7. Export as mp3, mp4, Anki

Export your flashcards as audio files (mp3), video files (mp4), or Anki packages from Flashcards. View exports in Exports.
