小明：你听说过那个商学院的新闻吗？

Xiao Ming: Have you heard the news about the business school?
小红：没有，是什么新闻？

Xiaohong: No, what's the news?
小明：有一篇关于商学院的文章说，研究人员发现商学院的学生判断不了假新闻，这是因为他们没有学到足够的批判性思维。

Xiao Ming: There is an article about business schools that researchers found that business school students can't judge fake news because they haven't learned enough critical thinking.
小红：这个问题好像很严重啊。

Xiaohong: This problem seems to be very serious.
小明：是的，产生问题可能会影响整个社会，但是很多人都不认识到这个问题，社会跟不上啊。

Xiao Ming: Yes, problems may affect the whole society, but many people do not realize this problem, and society cannot keep up.
小红：可是我怀疑这篇文章的标题是故意的，因为判断假新闻不仅是商学院学生的问题。

Xiaohong: But I suspect that the title of this article is intentional, because judging fake news is not just a problem for business school students.
小明：对，原因可能更加复杂。

Xiao Ming: Yes, the reasons may be more complicated.
