我在非洲做電商 (Document)

■ Target word (due for review)   ■ Not in target range   ■ Known word
2017.11.30广州杨涛.我在非洲做电商非常高兴来到一席和大家分享一点我在非洲做电商的故事

2017.11.30 Guangzhou Yang Tao. I am doing e-commerce in Africa. I am very happy to come here and share with you a little story about my e-commerce in Africa
在2012年到2014年的时候我当时还在华为公司

From 2012 to 2014, I was still in Huawei
帮助非洲多个运营商建立他们的移动支付系统

Helped multiple operators in Africa to build their mobile payment systems
当时也没觉得这个项目有特别大的意义但是后面发现这个系统帮助非洲很多国家的经济

At that time, I didn't think this project was particularly meaningful, but later I found that this system helped the economies of many African countries.
促进很大而且在全世界都有很大的影响力在非洲的三年过程中

The promotion is very large and has a large impact all over the world in the course of three years in Africa
我个人对非洲有了一个全新的认识之前对非洲的印象可能就是沙漠

I personally have a new understanding of Africa. My previous impression of Africa may be a desert.
动物世界贫穷饥饿和战争我相信大部分中国人还停留在这样的一个印象之上

Poverty, hunger and war in the animal world, I believe most Chinese people still have such an impression
但到了非洲之后我发现这里有非常现代化的城市也有很多高富帅

But after arriving in Africa, I found that there are very modern cities and many rich and handsome people.
也有很多黑富美广大的中产阶级也在蓬勃兴起

There are also a lot of black, rich and beautiful people, and the vast middle class is also booming
他们对生活品质的要求也越来越高非洲已经有12亿人口

Their requirements for quality of life are also getting higher and higher. Africa already has a population of 1.2 billion.
在60年代非洲开始脱离欧洲殖民者的统治

In the 1960s, Africa began to break away from European colonial rule
开始独立建国当时非洲只有2亿人意味着每15年非洲人会翻一番

There were only 200 million people in Africa at the time of independence, which meant that the number of Africans doubled every 15 years
按这个速度下去在三四十年之内非洲的人口会到50亿

At this rate, the population of Africa will reach 5 billion within 30 to 40 years
而且这是一个非常年轻的大陆我们可以看到非洲撒哈拉以南的人均年龄是18岁

And it's a very young continent and we can see that the average age in sub-Saharan Africa is 18
我们中国已经有37岁日本和德国已经接近47岁

We in China are already 37 years old, Japan and Germany are already close to 47 years old
从贫富来讲3个非洲国家人均GDP已经超过了中国

In terms of rich and poor, the per capita GDP of 3 African countries has surpassed that of China
有10个国家的人均GDP超过了3000美金3000美金相当于中国2008年的GDP水平

There are 10 countries whose per capita GDP exceeds US$3,000. US$3,000 is equivalent to the GDP level of China in 2008.
整个撒哈拉以南也就是世界上最穷的区域

The entire sub-Saharan is the poorest region in the world
他们的人均GDP已经达到1600美金实际上是我们中国2004年左右的水平

Their per capita GDP has reached 1,600 US dollars, which is actually the level of our China around 2004
这么来看的话实际上差别也没有那么大虽然整个非洲的经济处于一个蓬勃向上的发展水平

Looking at it this way, the difference is actually not that big, although the economy of Africa as a whole is at a level of vigorous development.
大概人均GDP增长在6%世界上GDP增长最高的是埃塞俄比亚

The per capita GDP growth rate is about 6%. The highest GDP growth rate in the world is Ethiopia.
超过10%但是我在非洲的生活和购物尤其是购物的体验

More than 10% but my life in Africa and shopping especially shopping experience
还是非常地糟糕当时购物的话有两个选择第一个是去超市和Shoppingmall

It was still very bad. At that time, there were two options for shopping. The first one was to go to the supermarket and Shoppingmall.
第二个是去普通人经常去的集市

The second is to go to the bazaar that ordinary people frequent
商超的话有两个问题第一个是价格非常高昂是中国零售价的3倍以上

There are two problems with supermarkets. The first is that the price is very high, more than three times the retail price in China.
这个还算是正常的10倍以上也比较正常

This is still more than 10 times normal.
第二的话选择非常少我在去年去拉各斯的一个超市去购买雨伞

Second, there are very few choices. I went to a supermarket in Lagos last year to buy umbrellas.
发现整个超市只有一种选择而且这个伞的上面印着超市的Logo

I found that there is only one choice in the whole supermarket and the logo of the supermarket is printed on the umbrella
我们乌干达办公室的同事买了一个投影仪用的幕布

Our colleagues in the Ugandan office bought a projector screen
京东价是270元人民币我们在乌干达花了3000多人民币

The price on JD.com is 270 RMB and we spent more than 3,000 RMB in Uganda
而且这还是一个二手的像商超的话可以说跟我们中国最好的超市

And this is a second-hand supermarket, which can be said to be the best supermarket in China.
实际上内部环境是差不多的但是它主要是为当地的富人和外国人服务

Actually the internal environment is similar but it mainly serves the rich locals and foreigners
普通老百姓去的更多的是集市集市的话让我想起我小时候

Ordinary people go more to the bazaar. The words of the bazaar remind me of my childhood.
大概2000年左右中国农村集市那种状态人非常地嘈杂

Around 2000, the state of the rural market in China was very noisy.
小偷特别多价格的话你要使劲地砍价

If the thief has a lot of prices, you have to haggle hard
不知道什么时候是个底如果你买了一个东西转身之后

I don't know when the bottom is. If you buy something and turn around
发现这个商品有质量问题你转过身来它的卖家已经不认了

When you find that this product has quality problems, you turn around and the seller no longer recognizes it.
那么我在想为什么为什么东西这么贵

then i was wondering why why things are so expensive
为什么选择那么少原因的话我分析两点

If there are so few reasons for choosing, I will analyze two points
第一个是非洲的工业基础非常地薄弱工业比较发达的国家还停留在

The first one is that the industrial base in Africa is very weak, and the countries with relatively developed industries are still in the
能够制造水桶拖鞋这样一个水平很多国家基本上没有任何工业基础

Many countries that can manufacture bucket slippers basically do not have any industrial base
设想一下我们中国生活的所有消费品都换成进口商品的话

Imagine if all the consumer goods we live in China are replaced with imported goods
价格翻三倍也可以理解第二个原因是中非传统贸易的限制

It is also understandable that the price has tripled. The second reason is the restriction of traditional trade between China and Africa.
做过对外贸易的都知道一旦商品离开中国

Anyone who has done foreign trade knows that once the goods leave China
基本上这个货就很难回来了再加上非洲买家在中国很难获得信用

Basically, it is difficult for this product to come back, and it is difficult for African buyers to obtain credit in China
很难获得授信基本上是一手交钱一手交货所以非洲的买家对在中国进货是非常谨慎的

It is difficult to obtain credit, and basically pay for the goods and deliver the goods, so African buyers are very cautious about buying goods in China.
一旦选品不利那么可能这个货的库存就砸在自己手上了

Once the product selection is unfavorable, the inventory of this product may be dropped on your own hands.
库存的风险非常大我在想怎么来解决这个问题

The risk of inventory is very high, I am thinking about how to solve this problem
作为来自中国的人作为来自未来的人很容易想到电商

As a person from China, as a person from the future, it is easy to think of e-commerce
通过这种客户的浏览数据我们没有必要把所有的商品

Through this customer's browsing data, we don't need to put all the products
都采购到非洲去然后再开始销售我们可以通过大数据做一个预判

They are all purchased in Africa and then sold. We can make a prediction through big data.
同时电商的效率和自动化能够大大降低交易成本

At the same time, the efficiency and automation of e-commerce can greatly reduce transaction costs
然后让商品的价格也得以下降想到电商我做了一点分析和研究

Then the price of the product can also be reduced. Thinking of e-commerce, I did a little analysis and research.
发现在非洲3G和4G已经非常普遍了马云今年7月份刚到肯尼亚

I found that 3G and 4G are very common in Africa. Jack Ma just arrived in Kenya in July this year.
他的感叹是肯尼亚的网速比中国还快

He sighed that Kenya's Internet speed is faster than China's
第二就是智能机的普及当前整个非洲智能机大概是30%左右

The second is the popularity of smartphones. At present, smartphones in Africa account for about 30%.
但是新出货的手机智能机的比例已经占到60%以上

But the proportion of newly shipped smart phones has accounted for more than 60%
也就是说非洲大陆处于一个快速的功能机转智能机的这样一个时代

That is to say, the African continent is in an era where functional machines are rapidly turning into smart machines.
再看支付实际上我在华为做的这个项目就是一个移动支付系统

Look at payment again. In fact, the project I did at Huawei is a mobile payment system.
当前世界上移动支付交易量最高的其实不是中国

Currently, the country with the highest mobile payment transaction volume in the world is not China
也不是日本而是东非的几个国家手机钱包从2007年就已经开始广泛地使用

Not Japan but several countries in East Africa, mobile wallets have been widely used since 2007
像坦桑尼亚尼日利亚和乌干达的移动交易量已经超过了GDP的50%

Mobile transactions in countries like Tanzania, Nigeria and Uganda have exceeded 50% of GDP
再看电商发展的情况我发现非洲活跃的电商公司都是

Looking at the development of e-commerce, I found that the active e-commerce companies in Africa are all
欧洲创业团队以及本地创业团队但是他们都已经获得了非常可观的风险投资

European entrepreneurial teams and local entrepreneurial teams, but they have all received very considerable venture capital
排第一的获得了超过6亿美金的投资前5名也获得了1亿到3亿不等的美金的融资

The top one received more than 600 million US dollars in investment, and the top 5 also received financing ranging from 100 million to 300 million US dollars
我想这些公司已经获得这么大的投入他们应该做得不错

I think these companies have gotten so much money they should be doing well
我刚开始的定位是说我要成为非洲本地的一个优秀的电商卖家

My initial position was that I wanted to be an excellent local e-commerce seller in Africa
这个时候我就做了一个艰难的决定就是从华为公司离职了

At this time, I made a difficult decision to resign from Huawei.
当然这个此处略去5000字家人的反对等等

Of course, 5,000 characters of family members’ objections and so on are omitted here.
做卖家要开始选货了我刚开始通过一些超市

As a seller, I have to start picking products. I just started going through some supermarkets.
包括本地电商公司的这种交流决定从电子产品开始

This exchange decision, including local e-commerce companies, starts with electronic products
电子产品我们就决定去宇宙的电子中心华强北去进货

We decided to go to Huaqiangbei, the electronic center of the universe, to purchase electronic products.
当时选了比如说这个耳机老板说你要进什么货

At that time, for example, the headphone boss said what kind of goods you want to buy
我们这里从1块钱一个的耳机到5000块钱的耳机都有你要哪一个

We have earphones ranging from 1 yuan to 5,000 yuan which one do you want?
我说我们还是进好一点的他说很多非洲人在我这儿买一块钱的耳机

I said we should do better. He said that many Africans buy earphones worth one yuan from me.
你要不要进这个我说我们做人还是得有底线我想卖好一点的

Do you want to enter this? I said that we still have to have a bottom line in life. I want to sell better.
然后进了很多十几块二十块三十块的然后进得还比较多老板就送了两公斤的一块钱的耳机

Then I bought a lot of more than ten yuan, twenty yuan and thirty yuan, and then I bought more, so the boss gave two kilograms of earphones worth one yuan.
结果货到之后发现十块二十块的完全卖不出去1块钱的很快卖完了

As a result, after the goods arrived, I found that the 10 yuan and 20 yuan ones could not be sold at all, and the 1 yuan one was sold out quickly.
后面发现因为大家都在卖1块钱的耳机非洲人觉得原来这是市场标准

Later, I found out that because everyone was selling earphones worth 1 yuan, Africans thought it was the market standard.
世界上的耳机就应该是这个样子的今天我们的地点在广州

Headphones in the world should look like this. Today our location is in Guangzhou
我们知道广州是全世界第三（世界）人民的共同的首都尤其是我们的非洲同胞

We know that Guangzhou is the common capital of the third (world) people all over the world, especially our African compatriots
常住的听说有几十万这几十万非洲人大部分都是非洲的买手和代购

Residents have heard that there are hundreds of thousands of Africans. Most of these hundreds of thousands of Africans are buyers and purchasing agents in Africa.
可以理解为非洲很多的村庄城镇它都会派一两名代表常驻广州

It can be understood that many villages and towns in Africa will send one or two representatives to Guangzhou
实际上广州在非洲的知名度远超北京上海

In fact, Guangzhou is far more famous in Africa than Beijing and Shanghai.
我们无论走到非洲各个角落看到中国人都会说GuangzhouGuangzhou

No matter where we go to every corner of Africa and see Chinese people, we will always say GuangzhouGuangzhou
因为他总有一个亲戚在Guangzhou

Because he always has a relative in Guangzhou
但是做电商卖家的这个梦想在大概三个月之后就破碎了

But this dream of being an e-commerce seller was shattered after about three months
因为我们发现跟当地的这些电商平台公司合作非常地困难

Because we found it very difficult to cooperate with these local e-commerce platform companies
账目算不清楚库存经常丢失

Accounts are not clear and inventory is often lost
