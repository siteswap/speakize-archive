The perfect tense is used to show an action that is complete and finished, or perfected. It is used with an auxiliary verb such as haber. You conjugate the auxiliary verb to explain when the completed action occurs.

SUBJUNCTIVE PERFECTS: haya and haya are both used to discuss perspectives on actions completed in the past.
